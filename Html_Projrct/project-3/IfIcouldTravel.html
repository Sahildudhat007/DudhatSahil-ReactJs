If I could travel anywhere

MOUNTABU

Mount Abu is the only hill station in the Aravali range, amidst the arid deserts of Rajasthan. Situated at a height of 1220 meters above sea level, in the Sirohi district of Rajasthan, Mount Abu, with its magnificent temples, cool climate, tranquil lake, exotic wildlife sanctuary and lush green forests, attracts thousands of tourists every year. Mount Abu is a magical retreat from the scorching heat of the deserts. the highest peak of Mount Abu is Guru Shikhar which is 1722 meters above sea level, offers a panoramic view of the spectacular city. Mount Abu doesn't fail to inspire awe in terms of cultural exuberance too.

VRUNDAVAN

It was in Vrindaban and its surrounding forests that the key events of Krishna's Mythological life took place, and as such it functions as a kind of heavenly world in which a religious drama unfolds apart from and transcendent of the normal confines of ordinary human society. Vrindavan was where Krishna was born, lived his precocious childhood, and grew into the attractive and intoxicating youth who would lure young maidens into the forest to participate in his divine play and circle dance.

OM-PARVAT

Om parvat is an ancient sacred Hindu mountain which lies in the Pithoragarh district of Uttarakhand, which is located near the sinla pass. This peak is very famous and is known by a variety of names like Adi Kailash, little Kailash, Jonglingkong peak, Baba Kailash and chhota Kilash. Om Parvat is somewhat similar to the Mount Kailash in Tibet.
